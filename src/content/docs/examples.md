Explore various ways to use the SEOCHO platform to generate specialized knowledge graphs.

## Analyzing Financial Reports
By feeding `fibo.yaml` into the pipeline alongside SEC filings, the Agent creates an intricately linked network of organizational relationships, asset acquisitions, and market correlations without manual tagging.

## Medical Research Extraction
Using strictly defined medical ontologies, SEOCHO can extract interactions between drugs, symptoms, and side-effects from unstructured clinical trial manuscripts, providing researchers with an instantly queryable DozerDB instance.
